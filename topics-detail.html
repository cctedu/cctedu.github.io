About us

About Us

CETM College of Theology (Formerly Referred to as CETM Bible School) is a World-Renowned Faith- Based Vocational Education College of CHUKWUEMEKA EGBERASE TEACHING MINISTRY-CETM and is incorporated in Nigeria, and is Certified in the United States of America By Association of Independent Christian Colleges and Seminaries (AICCS) Based in St Charles, Missouri, USA with the Name "CETM Bible University"; and it's Centre for Higher Studies is affiliated to ZionCity Theological University Ghana; and Kadosh Faith Seminary Naka Benue State, Nigeria is an affiliate of CETM College of Theology and CETM College of Theology is Accredited by the International Peace Human Right Education Council and is Assessed by the American Merit Council and in Association with the International Association of Certified Theologians. It's aim is to raise Godly Leaders, equipping them with sound bible doctrine. It's Registered Office is located at Okhunmwun Community, Benin City.

Motto:

"Raising Godly Leaders"

Vision:

The Vision of CETM College of Theology is:

- Equipping Christian Leaders for the Work of the Ministry - Ephesians 4:12

Aim and Mission Statement:

The Aim and Mission of CETM College of Theology is to:

- Raise Godly Leaders with Sound Bible Doctrine Who Would in turn Impart others

Statement of Faith:

We believe in God as the Creator of the Heaven and the Earth; And Jesus Christ as Saviour- The One Who is, Who Was, and Who is to Come, the Almighty [Revelation 1:8]. We believe in Salvation and His Finished Work on the Cross. We believe in the personality of the Holy Spirit. We believe that all scriptures is inspired by God and is profitable for doctrine, for reproof, for correction, for instruction in righteousness [2 Timothy 3:16]. We believe in Baptism by Immersion and Baptism of the Holy Spirit.
