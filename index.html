Beloved! Warm greetings in the Name of our Lord and Saviour-Jesus Christ; I welcome you to the official website of CETM College of Theology. CETM College of Theology is A World-Renowned Faith-Based Vocational Education College of the CHUKWUEMEKA EGBERASE TEACHING MINISTRY-CETM; and is a Certified Member of Association of Independent Christian Colleges and Seminaries-AICCS and it Centre for Higher Studies is affiliated to ZionCity Theological University Ghana.
